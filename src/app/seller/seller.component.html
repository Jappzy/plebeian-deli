Plebeian is integrated with Stripe to process payments.


When you click "Connect With Stripe" you will be taken to a special Stripe page to setup or link your account.


If you already have a Stripe account you will just log in and automatically be linked to Plebeian and taken back to Plebeian Site.


If you are new to Stripe you will create an account and then be re-directed back to the Plebeian Site when you are finished.


After the setup you will be eligible to put a price on your work and recieve orders!

You are an official Plebeian Seller!

You can now add a Price and Quantity to your posts. This will allow anyone to purchase to your work from Plebeian Deli.
