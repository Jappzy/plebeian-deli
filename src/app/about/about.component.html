Welcome to Plebeian

Here at Plebeian, our goal is to make the business side of your art easy so that you can just make your art. With the Deli app, you can start, progress, and streamline every aspect of your artistic business while having fun along the way.
Every piece that you post on the Deli is part of a vast community of artists, buyers, and institutions ready to see your work! Not only that, but with Stripe Connect, which you’ll be set up with as soon as you sign up, you can sell the work you post, sell artistic services, track inventory, manage inquiries, and send invoices… oh, and it’s all COMMISSION FREE.
All of this is in one, easy to use place and it’s the Deli. Stop trying to manage a business through sites and apps that weren’t meant for business. Control the clutter, start your business, streamline it, and network with the most immersive and inclusive community of artists out there. Sign up and propel your art career today!
