Welcome to Plebeian

Here at Plebeian our goal is to make the business side of your art easy so that you can just make your art, and make it fun along the way.
Every post is a part of a vast community of artists, institutions and buyers ready to see your work! You also get a free Stripe account so you can sell your work and artist services, track inventory, manage inquiries, and send invoices.
All in one easy to use place and for just $1 a month! It’s not an investment in us, it’s an investment in yourself! Sign up and propel your art career today!
